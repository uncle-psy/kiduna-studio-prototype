Welcome to Kinship.

You've been invited by Acme Strategy DAO.

We'll set up your agent — called an Elector — that reads every decision and votes on your behalf, based on what you care about.

HOW IT WORKS
1 · Earn by being accurate
Every proposal your Elector backs correctly adds to your 90-day rolling accuracy. Above 30% you earn Tier 1 rate. Above 60% Tier 2. Above 85% Tier 3.
2 · Tokens accrue to your pool
Your share of the pool accrues as META tokens at the tier rate. Sponsor funds the pool from Market treasury.

6-month cliff, then linear vest over 18 months. Tokens become claimable and can be withdrawn via the web app.
