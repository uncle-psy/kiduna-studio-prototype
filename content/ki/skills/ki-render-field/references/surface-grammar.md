# Surface grammar

## Field

Canonical always-live projection. Scenes hold stable identity; paired Portals join contexts; ACTIONS remain at objects; Ki is continuously reachable.

## Studio

Desktop working distance: Projects, materials, package inspection, Ally/Actor benches, collaboration, authoritative diffs, complex acceptance. No IDE or file-tree metaphor.

## Live

Mobile human distance: navigation, presence, capture, Relationship lens, simple approvals, “at my desk later.” No full Studio or complex grant/package acceptance.

## Express

Browser surface: quiet registration state, honest unregistered response, page/domain proof, provenance, and Ally-mediated open-web Actions.

## TV

Ten-foot shared-safe presence. D-pad/OK/back. No signatures, money, balances, wallets, building, or per-person accounts.

## HUD

Clear: Field leads. Context: balanced. Focus: HUD leads. Gold only marks a signed act entering/changing an Organization Record.
