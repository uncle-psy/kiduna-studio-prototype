# Manage Compute

Read [references/compute-and-ledger.md](references/compute-and-ledger.md).

## Procedure

1. Resolve Organization, human state, purchase/consumption type, currency, mode, feature gates, and account authority.
2. Read balances and amounts from the ledger service, never the graph or conversation.
3. Render disclosed raw intelligence cost, Agency charge/multiple, total, taxes/fees, and applicable Guest/Member pricing.
4. Create intent and obtain the registered financial confirmation.
5. Track payment rail settlement separately from local Action state.
6. Create Compute issuance/allocation only after the applicable settlement and policy command.
7. Freeze lineage snapshot where required; apply skip and reserve rules deterministically.
8. Reconcile ledger, graph references, outbox, and receipt before claiming settlement.

## Gates

Transferability, DEX activity, decision-market positions, lineage payouts, Curator allocation, and Sponsor distribution remain independently disabled unless tools prove activation. Architecture readiness is not authorization.

Simulation may demonstrate the complete economics in a simulated double-entry ledger. State plainly that no real funds or transferable asset moved.
